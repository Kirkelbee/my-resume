Channel Tunnel Rail Link

My role was as a setting out engineer, ensuring that the construction crews knew the lines and levels they needed to achieve with their timber, steel and concrete.

Some of the structures I worked on include:

  • Longham Wood Retaining Wall:
    A section of the rail was cut through between the M20 motorway and an unstable embankment. We had to cast over 150 enormous concrete piles with 1.2m diameters over 25m deep.

  • Burnt Ash pedetrian bridge:
    A small footbridge to maintain the public pathway across the new railway.

  • Hollingbourne Tunnel:
    When the new rail route passed close to small villages it was encased in precast tunnels and buried.
